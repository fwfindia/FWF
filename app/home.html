Membership Terms & Conditions

By becoming a member of Foundris Welfare Foundation (FWF), you agree to the following terms:

  • Membership Fee: ₹500/month, payable in advance. Non-refundable once training begins.
  • Skill Training: Free training is provided subject to availability of batches and slots. FWF reserves the right to schedule sessions as per operational capacity.
  • Project Funding Help: Funding support for livelihood projects is evaluated on a case-by-case basis and is subject to project viability and availability of corpus funds.
  • Member Network: Access to the member marketplace for selling products is subject to quality standards and FWF approval.
  • Future Support Programs: Additional programs will be announced periodically and are subject to eligibility criteria at the time of launch.
  • Code of Conduct: Members must maintain respectful conduct within the community at all times.
  • Data Privacy: Your personal data is protected as per our Privacy Policy.
