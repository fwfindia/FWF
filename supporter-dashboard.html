FAQ

What can I do as a Supporter?

As a supporter, you can participate in social activities, share your work on Social Book, participate in quizzes and events, and help FWF's mission.

How do I participate in events?

Go to "Events & Activities" to see social tasks. Complete tasks by uploading selfies, and participate in Fund Raiser quiz games.

How do I become a Member?

Sidebar में "Become a Member" पर click करें और ₹500/month membership fee pay करें। आपका account तुरंत upgrade हो जाएगा और नया Member ID email पर मिलेगा।

How do I contact support?

Use the support ticket form above, or reach us via WhatsApp or email. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.

क्या मेरे Supporter points/history transfer होगी?

हाँ! यह same account upgrade होगी — आपके सभी points, quiz history और social posts बरकरार रहेंगे।

Payment के बाद क्या होगा?

Payment verify होते ही आपका account Member में upgrade हो जाएगा। नया Member ID और password आपकी registered email पर तुरंत भेजा जाएगा।

क्या यह fee refundable है?

Membership joining fee non-refundable है। किसी भी सहायता के लिए Help & Support section में ticket submit करें।
