Our Process

From selection to a thriving business — here's how it works.

01
Select a Skilled Entrepreneur
सही उद्यमी का चयन
Our field teams choose beneficiaries with proven skill, strong intent, and an opportunity to build a sustainable small business.
02
Build the Business Plan
व्यापार योजना तैयार करना
We collaborate on a local-market plan, equipment needs, product pricing, and a practical pathway to early revenue.
03
Raise Fundraiser Support
फंडरेज़र समर्थन जमा करना
Donations are raised transparently and applied directly to machinery, raw materials, registration, and first-month working capital.
04
Mentor and Scale
मार्गदर्शन और वृद्धि
We continue mentoring, help open buyer connections, and track progress until the business is stable and self-sufficient.
